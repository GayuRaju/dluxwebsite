About Us

DLUX is a high growth Digital business that aims to bring the best to our customers, backed by our talented team, technology, and data. To achieve our customer 1st strategy, we have combined the Digital Marketing Technology and Services businesses to transform the customer experience. With an industry-leading technology team, backed by analytics, we are resourceful and willing to experiment. Our agile teams are empowered to innovate and deliver an awesome experience for our customers - no matter whether they choose to shop in-store, or online. As an inclusive, team-first company, our people are at the core of everything we do.
